Welcome to PROTEuS

We present the PROTEuS (PROTein Engineering Supporter), a database of contact conformations of the intra-chain and its neighborhood. PROTEus aims to propose point mutations in pairs of residues involved in an interaction, through structural alignments between the residues involved in the contact and their neighbors (considering the protein chain sequence). This site allows the interaction with the database.
